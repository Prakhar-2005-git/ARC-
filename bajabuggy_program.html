Program Overview
🎯

Program Focus

End-to-end Baja Buggy (ATV) development: chassis design, suspension, powertrain, electrical systems, fabrication, and real-world testing.

🔩

Hands-On Learning

15 days of Design & Dynamics training + 15 days of Fabrication, Validation & Testing. Real workshop, real tools, real builds.

🏁

What You Build

A fully functional All-Terrain Vehicle prototype — designed, analyzed, fabricated, and validated by participants under expert guidance.

📐

Tools Covered

CAD (Computer-Aided Design), CAE (Computer-Aided Engineering), mathematical modeling, welding, machining, and dynamic simulation.

02
30-Day Program Structure
PHASE 1 · DAYS 1–15
Design & Dynamics Training
PHASE 2 · DAYS 16–30
Fabrication, Validation & Testing

Phase 1 Covers

Introduction to ATVs & safety standards, chassis design & frame construction, suspension analysis & tuning, steering geometry & handling, braking systems, powertrain & drive systems, tires & traction management, CAD modelling & CAE simulation, mathematical modelling, dynamic analysis & performance evaluation.

Phase 2 Covers

Welding techniques, machining & assembly, electrical systems wiring & integration, battery selection & management, lighting & auxiliary systems, subsystem integration & full assembly, physical testing & performance validation, customization, off-road terrain simulation, final prototype demonstration & review.

03
Training Modules
01
Introduction to ATVs
  • History & evolution of ATVs
  • Types and applications
  • Safety considerations & regulations
02
Chassis Design & Construction
  • Fundamentals of chassis design
  • Material selection & tubular frame construction
  • Integration of chassis subsystems
03
Suspension System Design
  • Suspension types for off-road vehicles
  • Spring/damper selection & tuning
  • Impact of suspension geometry on dynamics
04
Steering & Handling
  • Steering components & Ackerman principle
  • Handling characteristics of ATVs
  • Tuning steering response for performance
05
Braking Systems
  • Disc & drum brake systems
  • Hydraulic braking system design
  • Brake force distribution & efficiency
06
Powertrain & Transmission
  • Engine selection & considerations
  • Transmission options, gear ratios & drive systems
  • Power delivery & torque characteristics
07
Tires & Wheels
  • Tire selection for off-road applications
  • Tread patterns, sizes & wheel design
  • Tire pressure & traction management
08
Electrical Systems
  • Wiring & electrical system design
  • Lighting, auxiliary systems & battery management
  • Integration of electrical components
09
Dynamic Analysis & Simulation
  • Modelling & simulation techniques for ATVs
  • Handling characteristics & stability analysis
  • Off-road terrain simulation & optimization
10
Fabrication Techniques
  • Welding & machining of ATV components
  • Full subsystem integration & assembly
  • Physical testing, validation & customization
