Program Overview
🎯

What You'll Learn

End-to-end E-Bike development — from concept and CAD design through simulation, fabrication, and real-world prototype testing.

🔧

How You'll Learn

Fully workshop-based. Hands-on tools, real machines, real materials. No slides-only lectures — every session involves making something.

What You'll Build

A fully functional E-Bike prototype — designed, fabricated, wired, and tested by you under expert mentor guidance.

This program follows a professional product development cycle. You start with design principles and CAD tools, validate using simulation, then move to the workshop for fabrication. You wire the electrical systems, assemble every component, and finally test and calibrate your complete E-Bike prototype.
