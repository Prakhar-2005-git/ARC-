Program Overview
🏎️

What Is SUPRA?

Formula Student (SUPRA SAE) is an international motorsport engineering competition. Teams design, build, and race a small formula-style car — tested in events like acceleration, skidpad, autocross & endurance.

🔧

Program Format

15 days of Design, Simulation & Analysis + 15 days of Fabrication, Assembly & Testing. 100% offline, fully hands-on workshop environment.

📐

Tools & Skills

CAD (3D modelling), CAE/FEA, vehicle dynamics analysis, CFD introduction, welding, machining, data acquisition, ECU basics, and real track testing.

🏆

Final Outcome

Complete Formula vehicle build in a team — from concept design through fabrication to track testing. Final evaluation & program certification included.

02
30-Day Program Structure
PHASE 1 · DAYS 1–15
Design, Simulation & Analysis
PHASE 2 · DAYS 16–30
Fabrication, Assembly & Testing

Phase 1 Covers

SUPRA rulebook & vehicle architecture, vehicle dynamics, full CAD modelling, chassis & suspension design, steering & braking, powertrain, aerodynamics, and CAE/FEA structural simulation.

Phase 2 Covers

Electrical systems & wiring harness, sensors & ECU basics, cutting/welding/machining, subsystem integration & assembly, static & track testing, costing, documentation, and team project execution.
