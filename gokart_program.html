This program provides a comprehensive understanding of the principles and techniques involved in designing, analyzing, simulating, and fabricating high-performance go-karts. Phase 1 covers chassis design, suspension & steering systems, powertrain dynamics, tire behavior, and design validation through simulation. Phase 2 moves into chassis manufacturing, component assembly, wiring, and culminates in track testing and performance evaluation.
